  • Chobani Facts

Chobani is a brand that is known for producks such as yogurt, milk, and other dairy products. The founder is a Turkish businessman by the name of Hamdi Ulukaya. It is the top selling Greek yogurt brand in the US. Chobani also operates the largest yogurt facility in the world. It's market share in the US is now more than 20%. The company is also known to give many of its profits to charity organizations. It also happens to be my personal favorite yogurt brand.
