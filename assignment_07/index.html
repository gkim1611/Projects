Final Project Proposal

The final project website will be a personal portfolio website. It will include content such as About Me, education, career, and projects I've worked on.

My intended audience is potential employees who want to learn more beyond my resume, family, and friends.

The design will be minimalistic, with one landing page leading to all the different pages. All the pages will follow a similar, uniformed minimalistic design. The color palette I will be using is primary: black (#000000), secondary: white (#FFFFFF), and tertiary: tea green (#D3F9B5)

Here are 3 websites that inspired my design.

  • Matt D'Avella's Personal Website
  • Studio Steve's Personal Website
  • John O'Nolan's Personal Website

I'm planning to have 6 pages:

  1. Home/Landing Page
  2. About Me
  3. Education
  4. Career
  5. Projects
  6. Contact
